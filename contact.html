Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some of the most common questions I receive from clients.

How quickly can I get a quote?

I can provide most quotes within 24 hours. For simple coverage like auto or home insurance, I can often give you a preliminary quote over the phone immediately.

Do you charge fees for quotes?

No, all quotes and consultations are completely free. I'm paid by the insurance companies when you purchase a policy, so there's no cost to you for my services.

Which insurance companies do you work with?

I work with multiple top-rated insurance companies including major Canadian insurers. This allows me to compare options and find you the best coverage at competitive rates.

What information do I need for a quote?

It depends on the type of insurance, but generally I'll need basic personal information, details about what you want to insure, and your coverage preferences.

Can you help with claims?

I provide full claims support and advocacy. I'll help you through the entire process and ensure you get fair treatment from the insurance company.

What areas do you serve?

I'm based in Milton, ON and serve clients throughout the Greater Toronto Area. I can meet with clients in person or handle everything remotely for your convenience.
